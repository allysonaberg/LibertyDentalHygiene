Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I get more pricing information?

All fees are based on the Ontario Dental Hygienists Association (ODHA) current Fee Guide. Please contact us for your specific quote.

Do you service workplaces?

At Liberty Dental Hygiene, we support local businesses to better support the health and wellness of their employees. Contact us to reserve your spot for one of our 20-minute employee health seminars or to inquire about our employee oral health programs.

What are your health and safety policies?

All clinical procedures as well as the processing and sterilization of instruments are performed with strict adherence to established guidelines set out by the Canadian Dental Association, the College of Dental Hygienists of Ontario, and Toronto Public Health. For more information or for a copy of our sterilization protocol please contact us.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept and process private insurance plans and Ontario Disability Support Program (ODSP), as well as the following forms of payment: Visa, MasterCard, Debit, eTransfer or Cash.

What is your appointment cancellation policy?

We operate on principles of mutual courtesy and respect. Your scheduled appointment is valuable time reserved solely for you. Please inform our office as soon as possible if you are unable to attend.
