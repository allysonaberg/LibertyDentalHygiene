We have been awarded the CMHA Peel Dufferin 2018 Caring Community Award!

We are excited to announce our mobile dental hygiene team directSmiles along with The Honourable justice Katherine McLeod are the proud co-recipients of the Canadian Mental Health Association’s 2018 Caring Community Award. This prestigious award is given in recognition of individuals and organizations that have helped promote and encourage mental health awareness, reduce stigma and have made positive impact on the lives of individuals and families living with mental illness.

Congratulations Justice McLeod, for your tireless hard work and valuable contributions to your community!

Thank you CMHA Peel Dufferin for hosting our services and helping to promote total wellness through oral health!
